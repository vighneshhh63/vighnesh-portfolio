🏆 1st Place Unity Horror 3D C#
Evil Mannequins
Summary

Evil Mannequins is a Horror Survival game built in Unity Engine. It won 1st place at the Gameathon at V.K. Krishna Menon College in 2026. The player must survive against mannequins that come to life in a terrifying environment.

Mechanics & Systems
  • Horror survival loop — player evades AI-driven mannequin enemies.
  • Custom enemy AI in C# using Unity NavMesh for detection and chasing.
  • Atmospheric lighting and shadow system to build tension and fear.
  • Full game state management — menu, gameplay, game-over, win screens.
  • Responsive audio cues tied to enemy proximity and player actions.
What I Learned
  • End-to-end development — from concept to polished, competition-ready build.
  • Building AI with Unity NavMesh and C# state machines.
  • Designing horror mechanics that create genuine tension and fear.
  • Delivering a complete product under competition deadline pressure.
Why It Matters

This project proved I can design, build, and ship a complete game — and win a competition doing it.
